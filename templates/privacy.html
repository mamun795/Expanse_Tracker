Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter yourself.
  • Usage data — basic technical logs (IP address, browser type) for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account.
  • To display your expense history and generate spending summaries.
  • To diagnose technical issues and maintain service reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services except where required by law. We may use infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please use the contact method below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided within the app.
